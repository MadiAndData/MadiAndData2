DATA CLEANING -
Nashville Housing Project

For this data cleaning project, I used a Nashville housing dataset. I standardized date formats, populated columns, removed duplicates, deleted unnecessary columns, and updated data values with case-when statements. I also separated addresses into multiple columns, such as: street address, city, and state. By performing these tasks, I was able to improve the accuracy and consistency of the data, making it more useful and easier to analyze. The resulting cleaned dataset could be used for a variety of purposes, such as predicting housing prices or identifying trends in the Nashville real estate market.
